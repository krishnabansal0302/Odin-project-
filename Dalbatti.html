Dal Bati

Description

  • Dal baati is a Rajasthani dish primarily composed of two main ingredients: dal (lentils) and baati (baked or fried dough balls). The dal is usually a flavorful lentil stew, while the baati is a hard, crispy bread made from whole wheat flour.

  • Dal baati is often served with ghee (clarified butter), chutney, and sometimes a side of vegetables or pickles. The combination of the soft dal and the crispy baati creates a delightful contrast in textures and flavors.

  • The main ingredients include lentils, whole wheat flour, ghee, and spices.

Recipie

  1. Soak the lentils in water for a few hours, then cook them with spices until soft.

  2. Prepare the baati dough by mixing whole wheat flour, ghee, and water. Shape into balls and bake or fry until golden brown.

  3. Serve the dal hot with the baati, drizzled with ghee.
