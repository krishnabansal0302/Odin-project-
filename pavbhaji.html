Pav Bhaji

Description

  • Pav Bhaji is a popular Indian street food that originated in Mumbai. It consists of a spicy vegetable curry (bhaji) served with soft buttered bread rolls (pav). The bhaji is made by mashing boiled vegetables like potatoes, peas, and tomatoes, and cooking them with a blend of spices. The pav is toasted with butter and served alongside the bhaji, garnished with chopped onions, coriander, and a squeeze of lemon.

  • Pav Bhaji is not only a delicious dish but also a versatile one. It can be enjoyed as a snack, a meal, or even at parties. Its rich flavors and buttery texture make it a favorite among people of all ages.

  • TThe main ingredients for Pav Bhaji include potatoes, peas, tomatoes, onions, capsicum, butter, and a special blend of Pav Bhaji masala. These ingredients come together to create a flavorful and aromatic dish.
