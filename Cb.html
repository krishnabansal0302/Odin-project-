Chole Bhature

Description

  • Chole Bhature is a popular North Indian dish consisting of spicy chickpeas (chole) served with deep-fried bread (bhature). The chickpeas are cooked with a blend of spices, tomatoes, and onions, creating a rich and flavorful curry. The bhature is made from refined flour and is typically puffed up when fried.

  • This dish is often enjoyed as a breakfast or lunch option and is commonly served with pickles, onions, and yogurt. The combination of the spicy chole and the soft, fluffy bhature makes it a favorite among food lovers.

  • The main ingredients for Chole Bhature include chickpeas, refined flour, yogurt, and a variety of spices. These ingredients come together to create a delicious and satisfying meal.

Recipie

  1. Soak the chickpeas overnight and boil them until soft.

  2. In a pan, heat oil and saut√© onions, tomatoes, and spices until fragrant.

  3. Add the boiled chickpeas and cook until well combined.

  4. Prepare the bhature dough by mixing refined flour, yogurt, and water. Roll into circles and deep-fry until puffed.

  5. Serve the chole hot with the bhature, garnished with onions and coriander.

  6. Enjoy your delicious Chole Bhature with a side of pickles and yogurt!
